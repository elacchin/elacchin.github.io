Elena Lacchin

Computational astrophysicist

Hi! I am a Postdoctoral Research fellow in astrophysics at the University of Heidelberg & University of Padova working in the Demoblack group led by Prof. Michela Mapelli.

I completed my PhD at the University of Bologna under the supervision of Dr. Francesco Calura . Exploiting the RAMSES hydrocode, I performed 3D hydrodynamic simulations of proto-globular clusters to shed light on the formation of multiple stellar populations.
